Tournaments

We compete in two to three qualifying tournaments per year and a regional and world tournament if we qualify. In these tournaments, members can practice driving the robot, coaching the drivers, and preparing the robot in between the six rounds on average. These tournaments are an excellent opportunity to test out our robot against other schools and enjoy the competitive environment.
