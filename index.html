Lynbrook First Tech Challenge

In Lynbrook FTC, we teach you the fundamentals of building a robot. From computer modeling to programming to hardware, we provide real world experience in robotics.

Learn More!

The Game

Computer Aided Design

Using computer modeling software (Autodesk Inventor), we design our robots before hand. This allows us to experiment with different designs and develop a design that can withstand the challenge.

View Repository
Hardware

We use tetrix metalic pieces and the complimentary electrical wiring to design intricate robots. In addition, we use sensors specific for the task at hand such as infared sensors during the autonomous stage.

View Hardware
Software

In order to program autonomous and teleop softwares for our robot, we use JAVA applications developed with Android Studio that can be compiled by android smartphones attached directly onto the robot.

View Repository
Other Tasks

We host worksessions on hardware, software, and CAD to teach our incoming members the baics of FTC, and we mentor FLL teams. We also have a series of youtube vides to teach these FTC concepts to others.

View Videos
Tournamets

We compete in two to three qualifying tournaments per year and a regional and world tournament if we qualify. In these tournaments, members can practice driving the robot, coaching the drivers, and preparing the robot in between the six rounds on average. These tournaments are an excellent opportunity to test out our robot against other schools and enjoy the competitive environment.
